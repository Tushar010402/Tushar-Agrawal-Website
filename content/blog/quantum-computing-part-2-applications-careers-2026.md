### What is the Quantum Internet?

```
Quantum Internet Explained
==========================

Regular Internet:
─────────────────
Sends classical bits (0s and 1s)
Can be copied, intercepted

Quantum Internet:
─────────────────
Sends qubits (quantum states)
CANNOT be copied (physics prevents it)
Any eavesdropping is detectable

Key Technology: Quantum Key Distribution (QKD)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
Share encryption keys using quantum physics
If anyone intercepts → you KNOW immediately
Theoretically unbreakable
```
