## The Government Scheme Discovery Problem

India has **100+ central government schemes** and **1000+ state-level programs**. Most citizens don't know which schemes they qualify for or how to apply.

```
The Reality of Government Schemes in India
==========================================

Total Central Schemes: 100+

Money left unclaimed: ₹1000s of crores annually

Common Problems:
├── Don't know which schemes exist
├── Unclear eligibility criteria
├── Complex application process
├── Information scattered across portals
└── Outdated information on blogs
```

**FOMOA AI solves this** by searching official government portals and providing accurate, up-to-date information in simple language.

## Major Government Schemes Explained

### PM-KISAN (Farmers)

```
Query: "PM-KISAN scheme complete details"

FOMOA Response:
===============

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Benefit: ₹6,000 per year in 3 installments of ₹2,000

Eligibility:
├── Small and marginal farmers
├── Cultivable land holding
├── Not a government employee
├── Not an income tax payer
└── Not receiving pension > ₹10,000/month

Required Documents:
├── Aadhaar Card
├── Bank Account (Aadhaar linked)
├── Land Records (Khatauni/Khasra)
├── Mobile Number
└── Passport Photo

How to Apply:
├── Online: pmkisan.gov.in
├── Offline: CSC Center / Gram Panchayat
└── Check status: pmkisan.gov.in/beneficiarystatus

Installment Schedule 2026:
├── April-July: ₹2,000
├── August-November: ₹2,000
├── December-March: ₹2,000

Source: pmkisan.gov.in (Official)
```

Success Rate: Higher with good business plan

Check PM-KISAN Beneficiary Status:

Method 1: Online (pmkisan.gov.in)
├── Go to pmkisan.gov.in
├── Click "Beneficiary Status"
├── Enter Aadhaar OR Mobile OR Account Number
└── View status and payment history

Method 2: Mobile App
├── Download "PM-KISAN" app
├── Login with mobile number
├── Check status and installments

Method 3: Helpline
├── Call: 155261 / 1800-115-526
├── Available: Mon-Sat, 9 AM - 6 PM
└── Keep Aadhaar ready

├── "Payment Success" - Money credited
